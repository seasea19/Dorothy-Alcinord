About me


Welcome to my website, My name is Dorothy . I am a Fashion-lover and life-explorer. I am passionate in learning new cultures and languages. I love writing my mind which only happens during my times of boredom. I write a lot of things, sometime pretty and deep but other times just wild texts about life. Most of my texts as you can see are either dark or sad. Why? because often I write during my down times. I also get inspired from people's hardship and my own for my writing. I listen to a lot of metal musics from which I get inspired from as well. Yeah, I'm a metal head. I love rock, metal, hardcore, and dark intriguing trailer music. Brief, Welcome to my world of words !! Hope you have a great time reading my crazy stories. All my texts were written with emotions. Whether real or fantasy, or whether you like them or not, each of my texts narrate a lesson or a hidden message. It's up to you to get what I am referring to. Get ready to have an intense and unforgettable dive into my imagination, it's a vast one!.
